About

Hi there, my name is Arlise Popruzuk and I have a huge passion fro art. I am a self-taught artist who has been creating art since I was a toddler. Art has been a part of my life for a very long time. I've always love being creative and art allows me to do that in every possible way. While I taught myself how to do most things within the arts, I have been studying art as well. I earned my Associate's degree in art in 2024 and I am currently studying design and will earn my Bachelor's degree in approximately 2028.


I have been painting with watercolors since middle school and acrylic since high school. I truly love painting, I could spend hours a day painting (and have)! Some of my favorite types of art to make are drawings and paintings, but I also love: sculpting, sewing, digital illustration, animation, collage, and crochet. Every now and then, I discover a new artistic hobby and add to that list!
